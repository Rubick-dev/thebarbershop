    About Pauline:

    Originally from Melbourne and with Italian heritage you could probably guess that Paulie is someone a little more flamboyant than your typical Coffs resident. A single mother of 6, she has done things the hard way and about 12 years ago she picked up the siccors and started to develop herself into what she is now. The best barber in town.
